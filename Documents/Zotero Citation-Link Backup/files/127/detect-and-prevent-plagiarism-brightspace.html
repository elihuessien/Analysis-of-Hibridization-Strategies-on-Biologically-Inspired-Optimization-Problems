Detect and prevent plagiarism in the Brightspace platform 

Detect, prevent, and analyze potential plagiarism, regardless of language, with our plagiarism-prevention partner for the Brightspace platform.

This automatic text-matching system integrates seamlessly with Brightspace to support K-12 schools, higher education institutions, and corporations in order to promote original thinking.

This partner solution for plagiarism prevention has been a great addition to our Learning Environment and the integration is seamless and very easy to use. Promote student originality 

This fully automated system for checking text originality and preventing plagiarism uses advanced machine learning to deliver test-winning accuracy and results.

The student experience starts when a student submits his or her document in Brightspace. The text is analyzed and compared to the partners content databases (internet, scholarly journals, academic publications, and student materials). Utilizing machine learning, the algorithm singles out potential matching sources for further comparison and analysis. The matched content is filtered for secondary and tertiary analysis to identify potential malintent and false positives.

When the analysis and findings are complete, the result is a comprehensive and interactive report generated for the Brightspace user.

Easy to integrate

As a simple plug-in, this integration provides an efficient and intuitive connection to the Brightspace platform.
